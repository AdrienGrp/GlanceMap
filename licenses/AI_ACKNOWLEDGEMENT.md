# AI and Creator Acknowledgment

Last reviewed: 2026-03-10

This app was primarily developed with AI-assisted coding tools.

We recognize an active and valid concern from creators and developers: modern generative AI systems are trained on very large collections of human-created work, while generated outputs do not provide direct attribution to every original contributor.

Our project position:

1. We support creator consent, clear licensing, attribution, and fair-compensation principles.
2. We prioritize open and clearly licensed sources where possible for maps, data, and software components.
3. We maintain user-facing credits and legal documentation in this `licenses/` section.
4. We will review and correct attribution if we missed a meaningful credit.

Communities and frameworks that address these issues:

- Human Artistry Campaign: https://www.humanartistrycampaign.com/
- Fairly Trained: https://www.fairlytrained.org/
- Partnership on AI (Synthetic Media framework): https://syntheticmedia.partnershiponai.org/
- C2PA (content provenance standard): https://c2pa.org/
- Open Source Initiative (Open Source AI Definition): https://opensource.org/ai
- Creative Commons (AI and the Commons): https://creativecommons.org/ai-and-the-commons/

This note is an ethical and transparency statement; it does not replace third-party license obligations documented in the other files.
