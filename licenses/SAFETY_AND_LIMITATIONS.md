# Safety and Limitations

Last reviewed: 2026-03-18

This app is a convenience tool for maps, navigation, terrain visualization and imported outdoor data. It is not a guarantee of safety.

## Important warning

Maps, themes, slope overlays, hill shading, elevation data, GPX files and imported POI data may contain errors, omissions or misleading interpretations.

In particular, theme or rendering bugs can hide, distort or wrongly suggest:

- terrain steepness
- path visibility or importance
- winter or hiking route information
- refuge, water or waypoint locations
- map readability at a given zoom level

Local conditions can also differ from what the map shows.

## Your responsibility

Do not blindly trust the maps.

Use your own judgment, verify with terrain conditions and other reliable sources, and do not rely on this app as your only navigation or safety tool.

You remain responsible for your route choices, decisions and personal safety.
