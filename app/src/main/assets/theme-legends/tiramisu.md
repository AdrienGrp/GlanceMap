# Tiramisu

Tiramisu is an OpenAndroMaps render theme for Locus, Orux, Cruiser, and other
Mapsforge-compatible apps. It focuses on hiking and general outdoor use.

Main map cues:

- Built-in styles: Hiking, Mountain biking, and Velo.
- Hiking, MTB, and cycling networks can be toggled separately.
- Contours, terrain, landscape elements, water points, huts, shelters,
  accommodation, picnic, camping, tourism, culture, food, shops, religion,
  emergency, and place names are controlled through overlays.
- The theme displays many outdoor POIs early, so dense urban areas can become
  crowded unless unnecessary layers are disabled.
- Hill shading is supported by the bundled XML.

Style notes:

- Use Hiking for foot travel, Mountain biking for MTB routes and tracks, and
  Velo for road cycling.
- Toggle POI groups off when the map gets busy.
- The author recommends current OpenAndroMaps V5 data for best results.
