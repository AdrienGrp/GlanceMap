# French Kiss

French Kiss is an IGN TOP25 style theme for XCTrack by Pascal Cochet.

Main map cues:

- Natural areas use textured fills for forest, marsh, glacier, rock, scree,
  scrub, orchard, vineyard, cemetery, sand, and water.
- Contour lines are drawn prominently for terrain reading.
- Paths, footways, tracks, and roads use different widths and casing to keep
  foot travel, tracks, and road classes visually separate.
- Trail difficulty and visibility tags are used where available in the map
  data.
- Waterways, boundaries, buildings, railways, bridges, tunnels, leisure areas,
  and airports have dedicated rendering rules.

Style notes:

- This is a compact topo-style theme with no separate style menu in GlanceMap.
- It is useful when you want a paper-map feeling and strong terrain cues.
- Route and trail details depend on the source map tags.
